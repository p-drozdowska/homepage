Keep active

It's important to keep moving and allowing time for activities that keep you on your feet. Here's some examples.

Exercise and stretch every day, even if it's only for 10 minutes.
Cook or bake something you've always wanted to try, but never had the time.
Catch up on those DIY projects you've been meaning to start!
Learn how to knit, crochet or sew. Make something to wear for yourself or a loved one.
